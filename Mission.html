Famous Space Missions

  • Apollo 11 (1969): First human Moon landing by Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin.
  • Vostok 1 (1961): First human spaceflight by Yuri Gagarin from the USSR.
  • Hubble Space Telescope (1990): Provides deep-space images and data for astronomy research.
  • Mars Rover Missions (2004–present): NASA's rovers like Spirit, Opportunity, and Perseverance exploring the surface of Mars.
  • International Space Station (1998–present): A multinational research laboratory in low Earth orbit.
